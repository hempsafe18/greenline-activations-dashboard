Market Intelligence

Field observations from March 2026 activations

🍇

Black Raspberry dominates. Across all locations, Black Raspberry 50mg was the top sampled and purchased SKU. Consumers responded to the flavor profile immediately — minimal objection at the 50mg dosage when framed around the non-dosed format.

🍓

Kiwi Strawberry feedback. Multiple consumers at Good Pour Longwood described Kiwi Strawberry as "too sweet." Consider this when planning sampling rotation — pair with Black Raspberry to contrast profiles.

💊

Drug test objection emerging. Noted at Midtown Liquor — consumers asking about drug testing. Reps need a clear, confident response: hemp-derived THC at compliant levels and individual sensitivity varies. Consider adding to rep training module.

💰

Price sensitivity at Good Pour. Consumers expressed hesitation at the price point. Signage showing unit price vs. cost-per-serving may help. Competitor kombucha and functional beverages were purchased by 3 consumers who passed on 3CHI.

📦

16oz format demand signal. Multiple consumers at Good Pour Longwood asked about a 16oz format. This came up unprompted at two separate interactions. Worth passing to product team as a market signal.

🏪

Midtown Liquor — zero competitor purchases. Best location performance this sprint. Zero consumers walked away to buy a competitor product. Staff were knowledgeable and supportive of the brand — strong reactivation candidate.
